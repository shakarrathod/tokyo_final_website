About Us

Tokyo AnalyticX, based in Tokyo, Japan, is a globally recognized leader in educational technology. We specialize in innovative IT programs and professional crash courses designed to build practical skills. Our mission is to empower learners with advanced tools, techniques, and methodologies that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We offer flexible learning options to prepare individuals and organizations for success in a rapidly evolving technological world.

Our Mumbai Branch

Located in the vibrant city of Mumbai, our branch serves as a hub for innovation and learning. With state-of-the-art facilities, we offer a range of courses tailored to meet the demands of today's competitive job market. Our Mumbai branch focuses on providing students and professionals with hands-on training in fields such as Data Science, Cyber Security, Web Development, and Artificial Intelligence. Each program is crafted to ensure practical application and industry relevance, enabling learners to thrive in their careers.
