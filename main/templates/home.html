Career Roles With Tokyo AnalyticX

Machine Learning Engineer

A Machine Learning Engineer is a specialized role within the field of artificial intelligence (AI) and data science. The professionals focus on designing, developing, and implementing machine learning models and systems.

Cyber Security Expert

A Cyber Security Expert is a specialist dedicated to shielding digital systems and data from cyber threats. They devise strategies, implement safeguards, and lead responses to protect against unauthorized access, breaches, and vulnerabilities.

Application Security Engineer

An Application Security Engineer is a professional focused on fortifying digital applications against cyber threats. They assess, identify, and rectify vulnerabilities within software systems.

Data Scientist

Data Scientists are experts at analyzing large datasets, building predictive models, and providing actionable insights for data-driven business decisions. Their skills in statistics, programming, and domain knowledge help uncover trends driving strategic growth.

Cloud Security Specialist

Cloud Security Specialists work on securing cloud infrastructures, ensuring data protection, and managing compliance within cloud environments.

Event Management

Event Managers excel in planning, organizing, and executing events, ensuring smooth operations and memorable experiences for attendees.

Spoken English

Spoken English courses focus on improving fluency, pronunciation, and confidence in conversational English, helping learners communicate effectively in various social and professional contexts.

Nail Artistry

Nail Artists specialize in creative nail designs, offering services such as manicures, pedicures, and nail art to enhance the beauty and style of clients hands and feet.

Makeup Artistry

Makeup Artists excel in enhancing facial features through the application of makeup, offering services for various occasions such as weddings, photoshoots, and special events to help clients look their absolute best, radiating confidence and beauty.
