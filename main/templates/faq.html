Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that involves extracting insights and knowledge from data using techniques from statistics, machine learning, and domain expertise. It encompasses data collection, cleaning, analysis, and interpretation to inform decision-making and solve complex problems.
▾
Data Analytics primarily focuses on examining historical data to identify trends, patterns, and insights that help organizations make informed decisions. It often involves descriptive and diagnostic analysis, aiming to answer specific questions based on existing data. Data Science, on the other hand, encompasses a broader range of activities. It involves not only analyzing historical data but also using advanced techniques like machine learning to predict future outcomes and prescribe actions. Data Science goes beyond descriptive and diagnostic analysis to include predictive and prescriptive analysis, often involving more complex and diverse data sources. It also emphasizes the development of models and algorithms to automate decision-making processes.
▾
Career opportunities in Data Science are diverse and expanding rapidly. Some key roles include Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, and Data Engineer, among others.

Ethical hacking involves identifying vulnerabilities in computer systems without causing harm. Challenges include dealing with ever-evolving security threats, staying up to date with new tools and techniques, and obtaining legal authorization to conduct security assessments.
▾
Basic knowledge required for ethical hacking includes an understanding of computer networking, operating systems (especially Linux), programming languages, web technologies, and cryptography.
